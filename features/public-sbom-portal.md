## Trust Center: Your Software Transparency Hub

Build trust with customers and partners through a dedicated public trust center. Share Software Bill of Materials (SBOMs), security certifications, compliance documents, and audit reports in a centralized, searchable platform that demonstrates your commitment to transparency and security.

### Key Features of Your Trust Center

#### Customizable Branded Portal

Create a branded, public-facing trust center that reflects your organization's commitment to transparency and security:

- Custom domain with CNAME support (Business & Enterprise)
- Organization branding and customization
- Searchable document library
- Public or sbomify.com subdomain (Community)

#### Comprehensive Transparency

Share multiple types of security and compliance artifacts:

- Software Bill of Materials (SBOMs)
- Security certifications (SOC 2, ISO 27001)
- Compliance attestations
- Vulnerability disclosure policies
- Audit reports and assessments

#### Automated Publishing

Streamline your transparency workflow:

- Automatic SBOM updates from CI/CD pipelines
- Version control and change tracking
- Release tagging and management
- Document lifecycle management

#### Enterprise-Grade Security

Maintain control while being transparent:

- Granular access controls
- Selective document visibility
- Audit logging
- SSO integration (Enterprise)
